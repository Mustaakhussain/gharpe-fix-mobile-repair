Service ke niyam

Booking karne se pehle in points ko ek baar padh lein.

  • 1. Final price phone model aur actual damage dekhne ke baad hi confirm hoga; booking form ka price sirf starting range hai.
  • 2. Payment repair complete hone ke baad, cash ya UPI se karna hoga.
  • 3. Agar spare part turant available na ho, to dobara visit ka time diya jayega.
  • 4. Booking cancel karni ho to kam se kam 2 ghante pehle WhatsApp par bata dein.
  • 5. Repair ke baad basic warranty di jaati hai (part ke hisaab se alag-alag) — details visit ke waqt bataayi jaayengi.
  • 6. Pehle se hue physical/water damage ke liye company zimmedar nahi hai, sirf naye kaam ki warranty milti hai.
  • 7. Customer ko address aur contact number sahi dena hoga taaki technician time par pahunch sake.

Koi bhi sawaal ho to booking ke waqt WhatsApp par pooch sakte hain.
